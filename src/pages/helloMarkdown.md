# 你好

今天过得怎么样？

Let $f\colon[b]\to\R$ be Riemann integrable. Let $F\colon[b]\to\R$ be
$F(x)=\int_{}^{} f(t)\,dt$. Then $F$ is continuous, and at all $x$ such that
$f$ is continuous at $x$, $F$ is differentiable at $x$ with $F'(x)=f(x)$.
